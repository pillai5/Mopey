What is Mopey?

Mopey is your personal online journal, where you can add entries daily. Theres no cheating, you can only add entries for the current day so it is an accurate reflection of your thoughts!

Mood Analysis

Mopey analyses your thoughts, mood-swings and feelings for easy goal-keeping and tracking.

Year Round Tracking

Mopey provides free, year-long tracking so you can keep your mental health in check.
